Learn More

  • What are carbon emissions? : Carbon emissions are greenhouse gases that are produced as a byproduct of burning fossil fuels, like oil.
  • Why should we care about reducing it? : Excessive carbon emissions negatively affect the environment. Everyone has their own carbon footprint which they can control.
  • What methods of transportation are best and worst in this regard? : The more powerful the method of transportation, it is more likely it will release more carbon emissions. However it also important to consider how many people are using the transportation at once.
  • Community Transportation Advantages : It reduces carbon emissions because you are sharing with many people. Some cons are it usually takes longer and it may have a fee attached to it.
  • Biking Advantages : It is a good form of exercise and it is very useful to get around in big cities. Some cons are sometimes it takes a lot of work to get somewhere and it is not that fast.
